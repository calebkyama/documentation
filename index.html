Introduction

Fruity loops studio by Imageline is one of the best digital audio work stations out there. Here is a brief documentation of the most basic sections and function of the software. We will tell you more about:

  • Piano roll
  • Mixer
  • Channel rack
  • Playlist

You can download the software from imageline using this link

Piano-rool

The piano roll is a simulation of a true piano . You can edit notes,write chords and melodies.It is possible to edit velocities of the individual notes to get a natural sound. It is divided in bars and the bars are subdivided futher into smaller subdivision upto sixteenth notes. One can also see notes as they play them on an analogue an anlogue sythesizer or any other instrument.

Mixer

This is where you mix you mix your instruments and audio files.Its also the section where you add effects like:

  1. Equalizers
  2. Reverb
  3. Delay
  4. Compression

The mixer has different view options such as wide and compact which you can choose depending with your preference.

Channel-rack

The channel rack contains all the instruments(plugins) and audio samples(such as drums). There are knobs on each instrument and audio file which you can rotate to specify the pan and the volume respectively. You can also group instruments for easy accesibility. The channel rack has a sequencer where one can input midi for linear instruments like drums.

Playlist

The playlist is the section where all your patterns are placed in tracks to make a song.You can also put other effects like automation clips. One can also directly place audio samples on the playlist.
